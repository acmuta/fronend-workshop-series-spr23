The elements are all the same height, preserve the neat column we were looking for, and the HTML source is easily readable.

Another thing to note is that by adding flex-flow: row wrap; (equivalent to setting the flex direction to "row" and the wrap behavior to "wrap"), our row of content automatically adjusts itself to the viewport.
